About Us

CGHT Capital S.L. is a European commodity trading group headquartered in Madrid, Spain. We specialize in structured bulk commodity trading across continents, integrating supply chain execution, compliance frameworks, and long-term partnership models to support sustainable commercial operations.

Our comprehensive trading strategy includes principal trading, inventory supported execution, and back-to-back trading, while maintaining commercial transparency and regulatory alignment in European and global markets.

Services

Commodity Trade Intermediation

Facilitating buyer-supplier trade communication across Brazil, Europe, and North America. Supporting compliance with international trade standards and commercial execution efficiency.

International Trade Advisory

Professional advisory on global trade regulations, import/export procedures, tariff policies, and market entry requirements.

Logistics & Supply Chain Coordination

Optimization of transport routes, documentation support, freight coordination, and customs clearance facilitation.

Contract & Negotiation Support

Market Analysis & Price Monitoring

Ongoing market trend analysis, supply-demand dynamics tracking, and global economic factors monitoring to inform trade decisions.

Risk Management Solutions

Advisory on hedging strategies, price exposure management, and trade risk mitigation for more stable operations.
