Crude Oil

  • API Gravity: Measure of density relative to water. Ranges from less than 10 (very heavy) to more than 50 (very light).
  • Sulfur Content: Typically limited between 0.5% – 3.5% depending on region and regulations.
  • Distillation Range: Temperature range where components evaporate, determining refined yield.
  • Pour Point: Lowest temperature at which crude oil will flow.
  • Viscosity: Resistance to flow, important for transport.
  • Asphaltene Content: Limited to prevent refining equipment fouling.
  • Water and Sediment Content: Strictly controlled for safe transport and refining.

EN 590 / Diesel

  • Cetane Number: Minimum 51 ignition quality requirement.
  • Sulfur Content: Maximum 10 ppm sulfur to reduce emissions and pollution.
  • Density: Between 820 – 845 kg/m³.
  • Distillation Characteristics: Controls vaporization temperature range.
  • Cold Flow Properties: Limits cloud point and cold filter plugging point.
  • Lubricity: Minimum 460 micrometers to protect fuel injection systems.
  • Quality: European standard EN590 specification.
  • Storage: Standard petroleum bulk storage conditions.

Bitumen / Asphalt

  • Penetration Grade: Measures hardness or softness by needle penetration depth under standard temperature and loading conditions. Commonly used for road construction and paving.
  • Viscosity Grade: Measures flow resistance at specific temperature. Commonly used for roofing, waterproofing, and adhesive industrial applications.
  • Performance Grade: Measures stiffness, elasticity, and thermal susceptibility under extreme weather and traffic conditions.
  • Oxidized Bitumen: Air or oxygen processed bitumen to increase softening point, durability, and aging resistance. Commonly used for roofing and insulation.
  • Cutback Bitumen: Bitumen mixed with solvents such as kerosene or diesel to reduce viscosity for spraying and painting applications.
  • Polymer Modified Bitumen: Bitumen mixed with polymers such as SBS or EVA to improve mechanical strength, flexibility, and adhesion.
  • Quality: International construction and infrastructure grade specification.
  • Packaging: Bulk, drum, or bag packaging depending on client requirement.
