Crude Oil

API Gravity: Measure of density relative to water. Ranges from less than 10 (very heavy) to more than 50 (very light).

Sulfur Content: Typically limited between 0.5% – 3.5% depending on region and regulations.

Distillation Range: Temperature range where components evaporate, determining refined yield.

Pour Point: Lowest temperature at which crude oil will flow.

Viscosity: Resistance to flow, important for transport.

Asphaltene Content: Limited to prevent refining equipment fouling.

Water and Sediment Content: Strictly controlled for safe transport and refining.
